About KUET Gaming Club

A future-focused community where gamers connect, compete, and represent KUET in inter-university esports.

Club Introduction

KUET Gaming Club is a student-led organization dedicated to building a healthy and competitive gaming culture. We host regular events for both beginners and experienced players across multiple game titles.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to develop teamwork, strategic thinking, and leadership through esports. Our vision is to establish KUET as a recognized powerhouse in the national university gaming scene.

History

Founded in 2021 by passionate gamers, the club started with small in-house matches and has evolved into a vibrant competitive platform featuring seasonal tournaments and cross-campus collaborations.
